Kongu Falcons MARUTHUVAR WELFARE TRUST

The Kongu Falcons initiative was launched in December 2019 with the goal of bringing together educated professionals from the Maruthuvar community to create a positive social impact.

Welcome to Kongu Falcons MARUTHUVAR WELFARE TRUST

We are a team of educated professionals from the Kongu region or residing there, working across diverse fields such as Medical, Veterinary, Banking & Finance, Insurance, Government, Pharma/Biotech, Legal, IT/Software Engineering, Core Engineering, Teaching, and many more. Some of our members are also entrepreneurs managing their own businesses. Our network extends across India and internationally, including regions like the US, UK, Europe, and the Middle East.

In January 2024, we officially registered Kongu Falcons as a public charitable trust and obtained 80G and 12A tax exemption status from the Income Tax Department.

WHAT WE DO

At Kongu Falcons, we are committed to empowering the Maruthuvar community by fostering education, career growth, and social upliftment. Our key initiatives include:

Scholarships for Students

We offer scholarships to meritorious and financially challenged students, helping them continue their education with confidence

Education and Career Guidance

We are dedicated to mentoring and guiding students to help them make informed decisions about their education and career paths

Job Referrals and Internships

We leverage our strong professional network to connect skilled individuals with job openings across various industries

Networking and Community Support

We foster a strong professional network, creating opportunities for members to collaborate, share knowledge, and grow together
