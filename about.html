WELCOME TO Kongu Falcons MARUTHUVAR WELFARE TRUST

Kongu Falcons is an association of educated individuals from the underprivileged community, either hailing from or residing in the Kongu region. We began as a WhatsApp group in December 2019.

Our Objectives

  • Unite and organize educated professionals and entrepreneurs for a well being of the society.
  • Foster networking, collaboration, and mutual support in education, professional growth, and other relevant areas.
  • Contribute to the welfare and development of the society in a meaningful way.
  • Serve as a catalyst for positive social impact.
